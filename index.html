Who Am I Now?

The Through Line
Who are you when the calendar is empty?
6 Things You're Leaving With
01
Your identity is not your title. It was never meant to be.
02
Transition is not a gap. It is a recalibration that most people never slow down enough to do.
03
Your values are the anchors. Everything you build from here starts there.
04
The discomfort you feel is data, not failure. Pay attention to where it shows up.
05
You belong to communities that hold you beyond your career. Name them.
06
You can hold three futures at once. None of them have to be decided today.
4 Questions to Carry With You
Q
Who am I when the calendar is empty?
Q
What do I value that has nothing to do with my title or output?
Q
Who are the communities that know me beyond what I do?
Q
If no one would laugh and money was not the constraint, what would I be doing?
2 Suggested Practices
◈
Find the Image
Pull one image, from a magazine, your phone, anywhere, that represents who you are outside of work. Keep it somewhere you will see it.
◈
Have the Conversation
Find one person who knew you before your current title and ask them: what do they see in you that has nothing to do with what you do for work?
